Food

  1. Lean meats: Cooked chicken, turkey, beef, and pork (with no seasoning or bones).
  2. Fish: Cooked salmon, trout, and tuna (no bones).
  3. Fruits: Apples (without seeds), bananas, blueberries, strawberries, and watermelon (without seeds or rind).
  4. Vegetables: Carrots, green beans, peas, sweet potatoes (cooked or mashed), and pumpkin (plain, not spiced).
  5. Grains: Cooked rice, quinoa, and oatmeal (plain, without added sugars or seasonings).
  6. Dairy: Plain yogurt (in moderation, and make sure your dog isn't lactose intolerant).
  7. Eggs: Cooked eggs (plain, without any seasoning)
